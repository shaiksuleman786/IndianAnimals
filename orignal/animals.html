You probably expect to find an Anaconda in this list, but it is not here because we use species names rather than group or common names. For example, the term "Anaconda" refers to a group of large aquatic snakes that belong to the genus Eunectes. They all are found in South America. There are four extant species in this family whose names are: Green anaconda, Yellow anaconda, Darkly-spotted anaconda, and Bolivian anaconda. None of these animals start with letter A.
